Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Simple Fitness is a personal workout tracking app built for iOS. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Data collected

Simple Fitness stores workout logs, exercises, programs, and cardio sessions that you create inside the app. All of this data is saved locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

Data we do not collect

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data from Apple Health / HealthKit
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties

Third-party services

Simple Fitness does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. No data leaves your device.

Data storage and deletion

All app data lives on your device. You can delete all data at any time using the Reset All Data option in the app's Settings tab. Deleting the app from your device also removes all associated data.

Children's privacy

Simple Fitness does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If the app is updated to include features that affect privacy (such as cloud sync or account creation), this policy will be updated before those features are released.
